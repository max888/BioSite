Portfolio

Here is some of the stuff I have done. Not all of it is hosted but all is available on my github account. You can browse my code there and see what you think.

Stranded Travel

Stranded Travel is a personal project of mine and the biggest to date. It is a travel guide aimed at the younger traveller who is looking for the best places to eat, sleep, party and explore in different cities. I created it with the idea of striking a balance between inspiration and information.

It was built on rails and uses javascript and jQuery to provide a more interactive experience for the user. I am using Instagram, Twitter and Google Maps API's to provide the information that is pulled in from the city name and GPS co-ordinates.

Beatbox

Beatbox is a music sharing app built on Rails. It lets users sign up and upload their music. Users can create profiles and comment on others music.
